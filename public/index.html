Welcome to Royalty Free Studios

We make music for videos!

(Or any creative project that needs music...)

That could be an online advertisement, a personal YouTube video, a theatrical performance, or school presentation.

Just like the name says, we don’t charge royalties.

With a one-time licensing fee ($20), use the song for any single project.

How long is your video?

We produce multiple lengths of each song to make editing a breeze. Bumper (3-15 sec) Snippet (15-45 sec) Short (45-120 sec) Full (2-5 min) *all lengths approximate, see songs for exact times

But you can always cut and edit our music however you want.
