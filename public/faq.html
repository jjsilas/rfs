Do I have to put the credit where people can see it?

Yes. Credits change from media to media - but in general a credit needs to be placed such that a person who wants to know where the music came from should have no difficulty in finding it. A reasonable effort may be expended (e.g. clicking on a credits option), but the credit should not be hidden or obscured. See the Attribution chart for examples.

Use a font size large enough to easily read with the naked eye, and display it for long enough so that the average person would be able to read all the information.
