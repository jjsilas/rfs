About US

Our goal at Royalty Free Studios is to make great music and an easy process for novice and experienced videographers and creators to use in their projects.

Royalty Free Studios, located in Dallas, Texas, is a small recording studio with a big mission: create high quality music for all levels of production with an easy process and low, one-time cost for the customer. Our studio is equipped with top of the line microphones, pre-amps, software, and outboard gear. Our production team is cultivated from long-time members of the local Dallas music community as well as photographers, videographers, content creators, and viewers. We know finding high quality music to use in any production for a reasonable price can be difficult… we are here to make that easier.

Royalty Free Studios production includes:

  • Shure, AKG, Sterling Audio, Sennheiser, and Audio-Technica microphones
  • Mogami, Hosa, and Pro-Line cables
  • Meinl, LP, DW, Treeworks, and Zildjian percussion
  • Hamer, Ibanez, Yamaha, Washburn, and Moniker stringed instruments
  • Roland keyboards, loop samplers, and drum machines
  • Lexicon, Morley, T-Rex, Moor, Korg, and Boss effects processors
  • Blackstar, Acoustic, Hartke, and Fishman amplification
  • Golden Age pre-amps
  • Yamaha mixers
  • Focusrite audio processors and pre-amps
  • HP computers
  • Steinberg’s Cubase Pro DAW
  • Native Instrument’s VST instruments, samplers, and plug-ins
  • XLN Audio VST instruments and samplers
  • Waves Audio VST plug-ins
