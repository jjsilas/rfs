Attribution/Credit License (Suggested Donation)

This license is for PERSONAL use only. If you are a freelancer, a business, plan on monetizing the material, or using material in advertisements, you must purchase a license.

The Attribution/Credit license is NOT valid for monetization.

Music is licensed under our Attribution/Credit license for some limited personal uses, which means you must credit our work by including the below information in an easily accessible location:

Song Title
Royalty Free Studios
https://www.RoyaltyFreeStudios.com
License (Attribution/Credit, Pro, Commercial, Broadcast)
It is important that you replace Song Title with the ACTUAL TITLE of the piece of music used. The Licenses page has a quick copy-paste feature that makes it all very easy!

If you don’t want to, are not allowed to, or cannot credit our music for whatever reason, you’ll need to purchase the appropriate license.

If you need the music in lossless audio format (WAV files), you’ll need to purchase the appropriate license.

If your project is covered by any of our other licenses, you’ll need to purchase the appropriate license.

If you are not sure which is the correct license for your use or if you want to discuss a commissioned piece or exclusive rights, please contact us and we are happy to help.
